Teaching Philosophy

Teaching is my passion. I enjoy working directly with students both in and out of the classroom. At the core of my teaching philosophy is my principal goal, which is to foster careful, independent and critical thinking among each and every member of my classroom community. I consider the natural curiosity that we are all born with to be the primary engine of learning. During the learning process, I let curiosity lead the way, and I view myself primarily as a facilitator of learning, rather than a lecturer who simply delivers information from a textbook to my students. There are four themes by which I translate these beliefs into action: Engagement, Empowerment, Enrichment, and Respect.
